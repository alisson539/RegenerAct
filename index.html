RegenerAct

Acting towards social and environmental regeneration

Explore

We are facilitators of regenerative practices.

The facilitator is like a teacher, but he approaches learning in a way that allows everyone to teach and learn. Our mission is to identify processes and facilitate their development, making use of relevant tools, methods and materials, and monitoring the active collaboration of everybody. Regenerative practices are content and actions that value Gaia's full life (the Earth as a living being of which we are a part), whether because they are related to ecology, collaboration, health, well-being, autonomy, or artistically promote awareness of these subjects.








Relevant topics:
- Nonviolent communication
- Resilient collectives
- Permaculture
- Agroecology
- Shared leadership

The sharing of this knowledge
happens through:
- Social media content;
- Courses and workshops;
- Private consultancy.

We think that this knowledge must be available to all, so we always seek inclusive strategies.
